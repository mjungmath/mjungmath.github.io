Currently, I have a junior docent / Ph.D. position at the Vrije Universiteit in Amsterdam. My Ph.D. is under the supervision of [Thomas Rot](http://www.few.vu.nl/~trt800/) in the area of differential topology. My general research interests lie in differential topology, differential geometry, analysis, computational mathematics and mathematical physics. Besides, I am part of the [SageMath](https://www.sagemath.org/) project. I particularly contribute to the sub-project [SageManifolds](https://sagemanifolds.obspm.fr/), and most recently I have added the foundation of characteristic classes.
